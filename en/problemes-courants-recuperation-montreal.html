Most common data recovery problems in Montreal

This page covers the situations most frequently encountered by individuals, SMBs and freelancers when a device becomes inaccessible or files disappear.

Deleted files or accidental formatting

One of the most common requests: a folder has disappeared, a drive has been formatted, or files have been accidentally deleted.

Unrecognized external hard drive

Very common among individuals, SMBs and freelancers. The device appears incorrectly, requests formatting or suddenly refuses data access.

Corrupted USB key or memory card

A small device but often an urgent request, especially when it involves photos, videos, work documents or unique files.

Inaccessible SSD

An SSD failure often escalates a routine case into a more technical one. It remains a frequent request when a computer or workstation becomes unusable.

Laptop that won't boot

Many clients don't immediately think of data recovery. They mainly want to quickly get their files back from a non-booting computer.

Broken phone with important data

A common request when photos, documents, conversations or other important data must be recovered from a damaged device in a personal or professional context.
