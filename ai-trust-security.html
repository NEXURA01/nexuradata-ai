NEXURA AI Trust Layer

Active public standard for the NEXURA website, operational assessment, AI estimate path, payment handoff and client status flow.

  • StatusActive for nexuradata.ca
  • ScopeAssessment, estimate, checkout and portal visibility
  • TLS/cipherTLS 1.2/1.3 edge encryption managed by Cloudflare and Vercel
  • Review modelAI-assisted draft, human validation required
  • Security contactsecurity@nexuradata.ca

01

TLS cipher protection

Public traffic is served over HTTPS with TLS 1.2/1.3, HSTS and edge-managed cipher suites from Cloudflare and Vercel.

02

Server-side AI handling

AI estimate logic runs through controlled server routes. Public pages do not expose service-role keys or AI API keys.

03

Secure payment handoff

Operational review payment is separated into Stripe Checkout rather than collecting card data directly on the site.

04

Human validation

The AI estimate is a draft assessment signal. Final scope, recommendation and next actions require NEXURA review.

05

Limited intake

The assessment asks for business contact details, current tools and workflow context needed to evaluate the request.

06

Public security contact

Security reports can be sent to security@nexuradata.ca.

What this trust layer means.

This page is a public operating standard and website security statement. It is not a third-party SOC 2, ISO, HIPAA, PCI or government certification unless a separate formal certificate is issued and published by the relevant authority.

  • AI useAI supports assessment and routing. It does not replace human review.
  • Cipher policyTLS ciphers are enforced by the production edge providers, not by client-side JavaScript.
  • PaymentCard payment is handled by Stripe Checkout, not by a custom card form on this site.
  • PrivacyPersonal information handling is governed by the public privacy and Quebec commitments.
