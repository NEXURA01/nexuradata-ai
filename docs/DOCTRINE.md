# NEXURADATA Doctrine

## Purpose

This document is the single source of truth for the NEXURADATA transition from legacy service positioning to a unified operational intelligence infrastructure platform.

The objective is systemic coherence: every page, visual element, workflow, integration, legal layer, and technical decision should reinforce operational maturity.

## Mission

NEXURADATA builds operational intelligence infrastructure for organizations that need clearer execution, structured workflows, controlled routing, and reliable operational visibility.

The platform exists to turn fragmented coordination into structured execution systems.

The long-term ecosystem vision is defined in `docs/ECOSYSTEM-VISION.md`. That document describes the eventual expansion toward operational memory, organizational topology, execution intelligence, operational entropy detection, decision intelligence, and an infrastructure operating environment. The doctrine in this file governs the current brand, product, UX, legal, and technical direction.

The experience should communicate:

- systems are active
- workflows exist

```txt
fragmented operations -> structured workflows -> controlled execution
```

The homepage must not rely on feature explanations alone. The interface should demonstrate the system through states, routing, dashboard behavior, and operational atmosphere.

## AI Philosophy

AI is infrastructure assistance, not the product.

AI may support:

- intake analysis
- workflow classification
- routing suggestions
- operational summaries
- escalation support
- audit preparation
- decision support

AI must not be presented as autonomous replacement for judgment.

Rules:

- AI remains embedded behind structured workflows.
- Important decisions keep human approval and operational safeguards.
- AI outputs should be explainable, auditable, and reversible.
- Failure paths must degrade safely to rules-based or manual handling.

## Legal And Trust Philosophy

Trust must come from clarity, not claims.

NEXURADATA treats privacy as operational infrastructure.

Legal and data-handling layers must align with:

- Quebec Law 25
- PIPEDA
- CASL where applicable
- data minimization
- transparent purpose
- controlled access
- operational accountability

Data collection must be limited to what is needed for intake, routing, workflow execution, communication, and operational reliability.

Use business contact language. Do not expose personal names, personal addresses, or private identifying details.

## Infrastructure Philosophy

Final target stack:

- Cloudflare Pages
- Cloudflare Functions
- Supabase
- Resend
- OpenAI

Supabase is the target stack for new development, but existing Neon paths remain temporarily. Migration must be progressive, non-destructive, and preserve current behavior until Supabase equivalents are fully validated.

No destructive rewrite should happen until parity exists for:

- routes
- workflow storage
- intake handling
- email dispatch
- operator visibility
- audit logs
- fallback behavior

## Legacy Transition Doctrine

The transition must happen in layers:

```txt
stabilize -> audit -> reframe -> replace -> optimize
```

Do not rewrite everything at once.

Legacy pages with SEO value should be classified before editing:

- KEEP: already aligned
- REFRAME: useful structure, wrong language or design
- LEGACY: old positioning still active
- REMOVE LATER: obsolete, duplicate, or conflicting

Data recovery and forensics pages should not be deleted abruptly if they carry search equity. They should be reframed gradually or retired only after a replacement path exists.
