FAQ

Before launching AI implementation

Where should we start?

Start with a concrete workflow: inbound demand, client follow-up, document production, internal operations, reporting or support. The first system should solve a visible problem.

Can this work with our current tools?

In most cases, yes. The goal is to connect existing tools before recommending replacement.

How do we control AI risk?

Through clear limits: minimal data, permissions, approvals, logging, testing and human intervention as risk increases.
